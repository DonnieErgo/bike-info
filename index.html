Road

On a road bike, you can ride on asphalt on different gradients: whether they are mountains or plains. The races take place in a team peloton, but you can also train on your own.

Gravel

The Gravel is similar to a road bike, but the frame construction is slightly different and it has wider tyres, all for riding on light off-road terrain.

TT

The TT is a triathlon or split start bike, and is only suitable for riding on flat tarmac; it's very fast and aerodynamic.
